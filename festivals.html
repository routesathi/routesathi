Plan your MP trip

Plan around the atmosphere

MP festivals, without the last-minute confusion.

A festival can make a trip unforgettable—but it can also change crowds, stays and local movement. Choose the experience you want, then plan a calm route around it.

Important: Festival schedules, access rules and venue details can change every year. The months below are planning cues, not a promise of exact dates. Before booking, always check the official Madhya Pradesh Tourism events page and the organiser's latest notice.

FEBRUARY · KHAJURAHO

Dance beside the temples

The Khajuraho Dance Festival is the cultural choice for travellers who want classical performance, temples, crafts and an evening atmosphere in one trip.

Best for culture loversBook earlyEvening crowds

Plan it right: Keep the temple visit in the morning, rest after lunch, then return for the festival area in the evening. Stay close to the Western Group if your budget allows.

How to plan any festival trip

  1. Choose the festival first, then select a stay near your first morning stop.
  2. Book earlier than a normal weekend; popular dates can fill rooms and trains.
  3. Keep one flexible evening and avoid a tight intercity transfer on the event day.
  4. Check official event, temple or venue updates 24–48 hours before leaving.

Want the quieter version?

Visit a few days before or after the main event. You can still enjoy the destination, but with simpler hotel choices, easier movement and more time at the monuments.
