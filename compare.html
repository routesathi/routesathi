START WITH YOUR PURPOSE

Don’t compare only photos.

A fort trip, temple visit and safari need a different pace. These short comparisons help you choose honestly—then each city guide gives the detailed route.

HERITAGE & RIVERSIDE

Which history trip suits you?

All are heritage-rich, but the pace is different.

FORTS · CITY ENERGY

Gwalior

Best first heritage trip when you want a fort, palace, food and simple city movement.

  • Best for: first-time visitors, families
  • Give it: 1–2 full days
  • Choose if: you want a ready route

RIVERSIDE · SLOW

Orchha

Best for calm palaces, temples and Betwa riverside time—not a busy city checklist.

  • Best for: couples, slow travellers
  • Give it: 1–2 days
  • Choose if: you want peace over crowds

Khajuraho

Best when temple architecture is the main purpose. Pair Panna only as a separate safari stay.

WILDLIFE & SAFARI

Which forest trip suits you?

Choose by forest experience, not a tiger-sighting promise. Safari permits and activities must always be verified before paying.

CLASSIC FOREST

Kanha

Barasingha, broad forest landscape and a calm 2–3 night wildlife trip.

  • Best for: first wildlife trip
  • Give it: 2–3 nights
  • Style: patient forest experience
Open Kanha guide →
SAFARI + FORT

Bandhavgarh

Strong wildlife identity with a fort landscape. Keep a full safari day and gate-side stay.

  • Best for: safari-focused travellers
  • Give it: 2–3 nights
  • Style: structured safari plan
Open Bandhavgarh guide →
SLOW & IMMERSIVE

Satpura / Madhai

For forest itself, water landscape and current approved eco-activities—not a rushed safari chase.

  • Best for: nature, birds, couples
  • Give it: 2–3 nights
  • Style: flexible and quiet
Open Satpura guide →
