Privacy &
travel disclaimer.

We keep this simple: RouteSathi helps you plan, while you remain in control of your travel decisions.

In short: RouteSathi uses Google Analytics to understand overall website use and a Google Form for voluntary feedback. We do not sell visitor information or ask users to create an account to read travel guides.

Information we use

When you visit RouteSathi, Google Analytics may collect general usage information such as pages viewed, approximate location, device/browser type and how visitors move through the website. This helps us improve guides and identify pages that need attention. We do not intentionally send names, phone numbers, email addresses or other personally identifying information to Google Analytics.

If you choose to send feedback, the information you type is submitted through our Google Form. Please share only information you are comfortable providing.

Google and other links

RouteSathi uses Google Analytics and may link to Google Maps, official tourism pages, booking platforms or other useful third-party websites. Their own privacy policies and terms apply when you use those services. You can read Google's Privacy Policy for more information.

Your choices

  • You can browse RouteSathi without submitting feedback.
  • You can use browser privacy controls or Google's Analytics opt-out tools if you do not want Analytics measurement.
  • You can tell us about a correction through the Feedback / Report an issue button on the website.

Travel information disclaimer

RouteSathi is an independent travel-planning guide, not a booking agency or a government tourism authority. Routes, timings, entry rules, prices, transport availability, weather, safari access and business information can change without notice.

Always verify important details with the official authority or service provider before leaving. Your safety, bookings, payments and travel decisions remain your responsibility.
