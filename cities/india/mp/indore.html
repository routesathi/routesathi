These deserve their own destination pages and full days. Do not add them to a city itinerary.

USE THIS BEFORE LEAVING

Small checks.
Big difference.

Check closure first

Lal Bagh, museum and show schedules can have weekly/seasonal changes. Verify before building a fixed day around them.

Check district listing ↗

Use old-city walking

Rajwada, Kanch Mandir and Sarafa are close. A short walk/auto is better than repeated long cabs.

Sarafa late, 56 early

These are different food experiences. Plan Sarafa at night and 56 Dukan in daytime/early evening.

Skip distant day trips if short

Do not put Mandu, Maheshwar or Omkareshwar into a one/two-day Indore page. They will get separate routes.
