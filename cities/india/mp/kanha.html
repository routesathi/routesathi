DAY 1

ARRIVAL

Reach your gate-side stay

Check the permit name, date, zone and reporting point. Sleep early; do not schedule a late-night road transfer before a morning safari.

DAY 2 · MORNING

SAFARI 1

Report exactly as your permit says

Carry the ID required for the booked visitor. Vehicle and guide arrangements/charges can be handled separately at the gate as per current forest policy.

MIDDAY

BEFORE YOU GO / निकलने से पहले

Small checks.
Big difference.

Read the current calendar

Seasonal timing, weekly closure and availability can change. Check the official portal immediately before booking and travel.

Permit zone is specific

A permit is for its booked zone; do not assume you can enter every Kanha zone with one ticket.

Carry booked visitor ID

Match every traveller’s name/ID with the current permit requirement. Confirm details before leaving your stay.

Respect the forest

No loud music, littering, feeding animals or off-route demands. Wildlife viewing is a privilege, not a performance.

Safari rules, entry times and fees can change. This RouteSathi guide uses MP Forest Department information; verify all live details before paying.
