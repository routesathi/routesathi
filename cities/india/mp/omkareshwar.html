BEFORE YOU GO / निकलने से पहले

Small checks.
Big difference.

Verify darshan rules

Official timing, entry route and special-day arrangements can change. Check immediately before travel.

Expect crowds

Monday, festivals and holidays need more time. Do not book a tight onward journey.

Use walking routes

The core temple route works best on foot. Wear comfortable shoes and carry water.

Keep the river safe

Follow local instructions at ghats and use boats only when operating safely.

Timings and temple rules can change. This route follows Khandwa district and official temple information. Verify before leaving.
