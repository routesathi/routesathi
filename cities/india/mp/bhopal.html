BREAKFAST · OLD CITY

Poha-jalebi and Sulemani chai

Start in the old city after shops open. Keep it quick: poha-jalebi is the classic choice; choose a busy, freshly prepared stall.

OLD CITY CIRCUIT

Taj-ul-Masajid → Moti Masjid → Chowk → Gauhar Mahal

Visit respectfully, dress modestly and keep footwear rules in mind. This is the right morning for Bhopal’s mosques, chowk lanes and Begum-era heritage.

Bhopal, properly explored

EXTRA DAY · PICK ONE

Nearby heritage,
without the rush.

These are not add-ons to a city afternoon. Give one destination its own day and return to Bhopal for dinner.

UNESCO HERITAGE

Sanchi Stupa

Best for Buddhist history and architecture. Start early; the main site is listed 8 AM–6:30 PM, while Sanchi Museum is Friday closed.

PREHISTORY

Bhimbetka Rock Shelters

Best for rock art and archaeology. Make this a separate south-side road trip; do not combine it with Sanchi.

BEFORE YOU LEAVE

Small checks.
Big difference.

Closures first

Van Vihar is generally closed Friday. Tribal Museum is generally closed Monday and holidays. Check live timings before leaving.

Where to stay

MP Nagar/Rani Kamlapati side works well for transport. Lake-side stay suits a relaxed scenic trip; Old City is better for heritage-food focus.

Use Maps, not guesses

Save each day’s route before starting. Bhopal’s lake side and old city are different circuits.
