BEFORE YOU GO / निकलने से पहले

Small checks.
Big difference.

Read current calendar

Season, entry time, closure and availability can change. Official portal immediately before booking check karein.

Gate is specific

Madla aur Hinauta ko same base mat maaniye. Your permit is the final instruction.

Carry booked visitor ID

Every traveller ka name/ID permit requirement se match kare. Stay chhodne se pehle double-check karein.

Respect the forest

No loud music, littering, feeding animals or off-route demands. Wildlife viewing is a privilege—not a performance.

Safari rules, entry times and fees can change. This RouteSathi guide uses official MP Forest and MP Tourism information; verify live details before paying.
