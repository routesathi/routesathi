USE THIS BEFORE LEAVING

Small checks.
Big difference.

Check Bhedaghat conditions

Boating, ropeway and waterfall access depend on water level and local safety directions. Verify on the morning of Day 2.

Check district listing ↗

Use one cab for Bhedaghat

Keep the driver through Chausath Yogini, Dhuandhar, Marble Rocks and shops. It avoids waiting and backtracking.

Safe viewpoints only

At Dhuandhar and Narmada, never cross barriers or go to closed zones for a photograph—especially in monsoon.

What to skip if short on time

Do not add Dumna or Bargi to a 1-day Bhedaghat trip. Complete the Narmada circuit first.

Timings and water conditions change. This route uses official Jabalpur and MP Tourism guidance. Confirm boating, cable car, tickets, weather and restricted-area notices before travelling.
