BEFORE YOU GO / निकलने से पहले

Small checks.
Big difference.

Read current calendar

Season dates, safari timing, closure and availability can change. Pay karne se pehle official portal check karein.

Permit zone is specific

One permit every zone ke liye nahi hota. Booked zone aur reporting point ko carefully read karein.

Carry booked visitor ID

Traveller name/ID permit requirement se match hona chahiye. Stay se nikalne se pehle double-check karein.

Respect the forest

No noise, littering, feeding animals or off-route demands. Wildlife viewing is a privilege—not a performance.

Safari rules, entry times and fees can change. This RouteSathi guide uses official reserve and MP Forest information; verify live details before paying.
