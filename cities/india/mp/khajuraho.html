MAIN VISIT · WESTERN GROUP

Western Group: Lakshmana → Kandariya → Devi Jagadambi → Chitragupta

Walk the principal UNESCO complex slowly. Kandariya Mahadeva is the key stop; also give time to Lakshmana, Vishvanatha/Nandi, Devi Jagadambi, Chitragupta and Varaha. Do not rush through just for photographs.

Allow: 3–3½ hrs · Ticketed entry can apply; confirm current gate and ticket details.

PLAN B · TWO DAYS

Khajuraho, properly explored

This is our recommendation. Day 1 gives Western Group its time; Day 2 links the Eastern/Jain and Southern groups without forcing a rushed temple day.

2 full daysMost balanced plan

DAY 1 · Western Group

  1. 08:00Breakfast near Western GroupBegin early and walk into the main complex.
  2. 08:45Western Group monumentsLakshmana, Kandariya Mahadeva, Vishvanatha/Nandi, Devi Jagadambi, Chitragupta and Varaha. Allow 3½–4 hours.

PLAN C · EXTRA DAY

Nature, not another rushed route

Keep the two temple days unchanged. On an extra day choose only one: Raneh Falls/Ken Gharial Sanctuary or a booked Panna Tiger Reserve safari.

1 extra dayNature outing
OPTION 1

Raneh Falls

Use a half/full day for the canyon and falls. It is around 22 km from Khajuraho; conditions and water flow vary by season.

OPTION 2

Panna Tiger Reserve

Choose this only with a confirmed safari permit/slot. It is not a casual temple-evening add-on.

EVENING

Return to Khajuraho

Keep the evening free for dinner, packing or a local cultural programme if officially confirmed for your date.

Why 2 days is best:You see all three temple groups properly. Raneh Falls and Panna are rewarding, but they deserve their own day.

USE THIS BEFORE LEAVING

Small checks.
Big difference.

Western Group first

The Western Group is the key UNESCO complex and works best in the morning. Confirm current entry gate, ticket and closure details before leaving.

Check UNESCO details ↗

Temple etiquette

These are active sacred spaces as well as monuments. Dress respectfully, remove shoes where required and follow photography rules at each location.

Use one cab for Day 2

Eastern/Jain and Southern groups are best done in one vehicle route. Ask the driver to wait, or agree on the complete route before starting.

Start navigation ↗

Nature is a separate day

Do not add Raneh Falls or Panna after a full temple day. Check safari booking, seasonal access and weather directly before planning it.
