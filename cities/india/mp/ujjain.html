DO THIS BEFORE YOU TRAVEL

Mahakal booking,
not guesswork.

Use only official booking

Bhasma Aarti, Sheeghra Darshan and other aarti services must be checked on the temple’s official portal. Do not depend on agents or old screenshots.

Open official Mahakal services ↗

Bhasma Aarti is separate

The official temple schedule lists Bhasma Aarti around 4–6 AM. If you attend it, rest after breakfast and do not pack another long early-morning line into the same day.

Check today’s official timings ↗

Festival crowd changes everything

Monday, Shravan, Mahashivratri, Sawari and Simhastha days can change queue, parking and route access. Keep a buffer and verify on the official portal.

Stay near the core

For a first trip, stay around Mahakal Road, Freeganj or the station side. You can return easily after evening Ram Ghat or Mahakal Lok.

BEFORE YOU LEAVE

Small checks.
Peaceful darshan.

Keep your booking proof

Save confirmation, ID and official helpdesk details offline. Do not share OTPs with any agent.

Temple comfort

Wear modest, comfortable clothing and footwear that is easy to remove. Carry water but follow temple security rules.

Use one outer-route cab

For Day 2, agree the complete route and waiting time before starting. Do not depend on one-way autos between distant temples.
