BEFORE YOU GO / निकलने से पहले

Small checks.
Big difference.

Check current season

Madhai/Churna core tourism and activities can change by season and weather. Verify live before travel.

Permit is specific

Permit is for its booked round/area; do not assume it allows every Satpura gate or activity.

Carry booked visitor ID

Every traveller name/ID must match the permit requirement. Check before leaving your stay.

Respect the forest

No noise, littering, feeding animals or off-route demands. Quiet travel is part of Satpura’s experience.

Safari rules, activity availability, entry times and fees can change. This RouteSathi guide uses official MP Forest and MP Tourism information; verify live details before paying.
