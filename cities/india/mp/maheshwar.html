USE THIS BEFORE LEAVING

Small checks.
Big difference.

River activity is conditional

Boat rides depend on weather and local operation. Confirm on the same day and never enter closed areas.

Walk the core

Fort, temples and ghats are best on foot. Keep comfortable shoes and water.

Shop handloom thoughtfully

Ask about fabric, weaving and care. Buy only what you can carry safely onward.

Do not combine destinations

Indore, Mandu and Omkareshwar are separate routes. Maheshwar deserves its own one-night rhythm.

Timings and river conditions change. This route follows MP Tourism and Khargone district guidance. Confirm boat operation, tickets and weather before travelling.
