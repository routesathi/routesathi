BEFORE YOU GO / निकलने से पहले

Small checks.
Big difference.

Read current calendar

Season, entry time, weekly closure and availability can change. Official portal immediately before booking check karein.

Gate is specific

Turia, Karmajhiri and Jamtara ko same base mat maaniye. Your permit is the final instruction.

Carry booked visitor ID

Every traveller ka name/ID permit requirement se match kare. Stay chhodne se pehle double-check karein.

Respect the forest

No loud music, littering, feeding animals or off-route demands. Safari wildlife ke liye hai, performance ke liye nahi.

Safari rules, entry times and fees can change. This RouteSathi guide uses MP Forest and MP Tourism information; verify live details before paying.
