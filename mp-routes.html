READY-MADE MP ROUTES

More than one city?
Follow the flow.

These routes leave proper time for transfers, rest and actual sightseeing. Use them as a starting point; open every city guide for detailed daily routes and current checks.

5 DAYS · HERITAGE FIRST

Gwalior → Orchha → Khajuraho

Fort city, calm riverside palaces and temple art—best north MP introduction.

Best for: families, couples, first heritage trip

DAY 1Gwalior fort + city
DAY 2Gwalior morning, transfer to Orchha
DAY 3Orchha palaces, temples, Betwa
DAY 4–5Transfer to Khajuraho + temple day
Do not add Bandhavgarh unless you have at least two more nights.Start with Gwalior guide →
6 DAYS · MALWA CIRCUIT

Indore → Ujjain → Omkareshwar → Maheshwar → Mandu

Food, Jyotirlinga, Narmada ghats and royal ruins—keep the transfer days light.

Best for: culture, spiritual travel, food lovers

DAY 1Arrive Indore, transfer to Ujjain
DAY 2Ujjain darshan day
DAY 3Omkareshwar, then Maheshwar stay
DAY 4–6Maheshwar, Mandu, return to Indore
Use darshan timing to plan this route—not a fixed old itinerary screenshot.Start with Indore guide →
7 DAYS · LAKES, HILLS & FOREST

Bhopal → Sanchi → Pachmarhi → Satpura / Madhai

City culture, UNESCO-era heritage, hill station and a slow wildlife finish.

Best for: families, nature, first long MP trip

DAY 1Bhopal lakes + museums
DAY 2Sanchi day trip, return Bhopal
DAY 3–4Bhimbetka route + Pachmarhi stay
DAY 5–7Transfer to Satpura, safari, return
Keep Pachmarhi sightseeing and Satpura safari as different experiences.Start with Bhopal guide →
3 DAYS · TEMPLES + WILDLIFE

Khajuraho → Panna

A short but balanced route: temple art first, then a proper gate-side Panna safari stay.

Best for: couples, art lovers, short wildlife add-on

DAY 1Khajuraho Western Group
DAY 2Transfer to Madla side + permit check
DAY 3Safari windows or return after breakfast
ADD NIGHTBest for two safari windows
Never force Panna safari into a packed Khajuraho sightseeing day.Open Panna guide →
4 DAYS · RIVER & ROYAL MP

Indore → Mandu → Maheshwar

A shorter version for travellers who want Mandu monuments and Maheshwar sunset without a temple circuit.

Best for: couples, weekend road trip, heritage

DAY 1Indore arrival + food evening
DAY 2Transfer to Mandu, sunset viewpoint
DAY 3Mandu monuments, transfer to Maheshwar
DAY 4Ghat sunrise + return Indore
Monsoon views are lovely, but always keep rain and road buffer.Open Mandu guide →
4 DAYS · WILDLIFE + RIVER

Jabalpur → Bandhavgarh → Jabalpur

Use Jabalpur as the transport base and Bandhavgarh as the real wildlife stay—not a same-day safari attempt.

Best for: safari-focused travellers

DAY 1Arrive Jabalpur, rest or Bhedaghat
DAY 2Transfer to Bandhavgarh gate-side stay
DAY 3Confirmed safari windows
DAY 4Return Jabalpur after breakfast
Safari permit, guide and gate reporting details must be verified before travel.Open Bandhavgarh guide →
